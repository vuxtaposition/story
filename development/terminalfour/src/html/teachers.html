What is the story of your stuff?

It’s a great project, particularly for transition year students, but secondary school students of any age can get involved. It is designed to be a fun and interactive competition that is also very educational.

Did you know

The average American home has 300,000 items in it! That’s a LOT of stuff and we’re probably not far off that number here in Ireland

Encourage your students to get thinking creatively about a meaningul subject

By its nature this project encourages students to get thinking creatively about a meaningful subject – the lifecycle of everyday products and how this impacts upon the environment.

Prizes for students and the school

Not only will the winning student receive a €500 prize, we’re also giving away €500 more to their school and a workshop in video creation from Christian Tierney himself!
